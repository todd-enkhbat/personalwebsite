Astropreneur

Building the bridge between space science and venture.

Todd Enkhbat

Mongolian-born Columbia astrophysics student and founder. From black hole imaging to rocketry and edtech, Todd blends research with execution to make humanity multi-planetary. A John Jay Scholar (top 0.03%), he pursues an astrophysics major with philosophy and business focus.

His work spans from co-authoring a NASA NIAC-funded mission to capture real-time black hole video, to engineering ablative thermal liners for collegiate hybrid rocket engines. The same instincts that guide herders across harsh Mongolian landscapes guide his approach to space technology and venture-building.

Having built $800K+ in teen ventures, raised $5.8M in commitments for a Mars analog habitat, and launched an edtech platform uplifting Mongolian students, Todd operates at the intersection of rigorous science and entrepreneurial execution.
